The Passionate and the Lazy: A Bionote

Andre Gianne G. Gamez is a student of Bachelor of Science in Computer Engineering at University of the East - Manila Campus. Driven by passion and craziness, he strives hard to know every crevice that this field of study has to offer. He is known by his peers at his inherent impulsiveness and laziness, and with his expansive knowledge with the topic or subject they take. He can be called as the "jack of all trades, master of none" or as a generalist.

Currently pursuing his career path as an Embedded Systems Developer, he has to learn a lot of programming and electronics to be successful in the near future. Having possession of a lot of interests including reading and writing literature, visual arts, programming, exploring, and learning new things, he certainly could do anything that he has been into.

With his curiosity in cloud nine, his laziness also brings him down back to even footing.

Currently, he has plans to pursue a Master's Degree on Computer Engineering, and also to pursue another Bachelor's Degree in Mechanical Engineering or Mechatronics Engineering.

Surviving in a tech-driven world needs continuous learning.
